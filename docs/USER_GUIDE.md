# TurnOver User Guide

## Quick Start

### First Time Setup
1. **Open the app** - You'll see the Capture tab
2. **Go to Settings** - Tap the gear icon
3. **Enter your OpenAI API key** - Required for AI analysis
4. **Optional: Enable Level Lock** - For straighter photos

### Your First Item

1. **Capture Photos**
   - Tap "Add Photo" 
   - Take 1-10 photos of your item
   - More angles = better AI analysis

2. **Analyze with AI**
   - Tap "Analyze with AI"
   - Wait for results (5-15 seconds)
   - Review: name, category, condition, estimated value

3. **Choose Your Path**
   - **SELL** ‚Üí Select marketplaces ‚Üí Get listing text
   - **DONATE** ‚Üí Select charity ‚Üí Get tax receipt

### üì∑ Photo Capture

**Standard Mode**
- Tap "Add Photo"

**Level Lock Mode** (Optional)

### ü§ñ AI Analysis

The AI examines your photos and provides:

- **Category** - Clothing, Electronics, Furniture, etc.
- **Condition** - Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor
- **Estimated Value** - Fair market value in USD
- **Confidence Score** - How certain the AI is

4. Copy or share the listing text

## Tips for Best Results

### Taking Photos
‚úÖ Good lighting (natural light best)
‚úÖ Clean, uncluttered background
‚úÖ Multiple angles (front, back, sides)
‚úÖ Close-ups of details, labels, damage
‚úÖ Use Level Lock for flat items

‚ùå Avoid blurry photos
‚ùå Avoid harsh shadows
‚ùå Don't crop out important details

### Getting Accurate Values
- Include brand labels in photos
- Show condition clearly (wear, damage)
- Photograph any accessories included
- Take close-ups of serial numbers for electronics

### Tax Deductions
- Keep all donation receipts
- Items over $500: Keep detailed records
- Items over $5,000: Get professional appraisal
- Consult a tax professional for eligibility

## Troubleshooting

### "Analysis Failed"
- Check your internet connection
- Verify your API key in Settings
- Try with fewer/different photos

### Camera Not Working
- Check camera permissions in iOS Settings
- Restart the app
- On simulator: Use photo library instead

### Listings Not Copying
- Ensure you've selected at least one platform
- Try the Share option instead of Copy
